Data Visualization Generator
Big Data is an ever-growing source of information, with governments and corporations generating more Big Data than ever. It proves to be extremely valuable for companies and governments to be able to extract trends and patterns from this data and use this information to better prepare and/or optimize any services they offer.

This repository represents the visualisation of Big Data. However, the catch comes in where, instead of manually creating these visualisations of Big Data, an Interactive Genetic Algorithm (IGA) will be implemented and let the user be able to select/choose the best visualisation of the given data, be it a graph/chart/scatter plot etc.
Prometheus' Puzzles
Puzzles have been a hobby of many people over the centuries, coming invarious forms which have various ways of solving. Puzzles have played a role in people’s problem solving skills. Puzzle generators have allowed for more puzzles to be created, using various techniques to create interesting and challenging puzzles.

Our system is a puzzle generator website which allows users to create tiling puzzles using AI or manual generation, share created puzzles, rate puzzles shared by other users and additionally solve created puzzles. The user should ultimately be able to download a 3D printable file of the puzzle and print it in order to solve the puzzle in their own space and time.

The Ornament Short Film
The film starts off introducing John, a student working the night before his birthday. John received an African sculpture ornament as a gift from his aunt. The ornament is on his book shelf. After his friends called– Michael and Ezekiel – the ornament quickly became the topic of discussion. Micheal found a link to a webpage explaining the ominous meaning behind the ornament. John thought of it as nothing more than a joke, but what he did not know, is that it would mark the beginning of a dreadful evening.

John prepares orders himself a pizza, as he does, the ornament starts moving ever so slightly. John plops down in front of his computer. After a little while, John receives a text message from an unknown number. Nothing is said on the message, it just contains an upside-down smiley emoji. John shrugs it off and continues working. He then receives another message, more eerie than the last. John immediately blocks the number. But that was not the last he would hear from them.

John inspects the kitchen after he herd questionable noises. As the tension rises. The pizza man knocks on the front door. John answers the knock and pays for his pizza. Suddenly, Ezekiel jumps in front of John at an attempt of scaring John. Ezekiel wanted to give John his birthday gift.

John is put at ease as he realizes Ezekiel is also responsible for the unknown text messages. The relief he feels from this encounter is short lived, for shortly after he meets the true culprit.
